Privacy Policy

This page explains how SparkIt handles website, contact form, advertising, and lead information.

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

SparkIt Design, also referred to as SparkIt, respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share information when you visit our website, submit a contact form, respond to an ad, or communicate with us about SparkIt services.

Information we collect

SparkIt may collect basic information you choose to provide, including your name, business name, email address, phone number, website, social media links, project details, service interests, budget information, and message content.

If you submit information through a Meta or Facebook lead form, Instagram lead form, website contact form, or other advertising form, we may receive the information you choose to provide through that form. This may include your name, email address, phone number, business information, and any additional details you submit.

How we use information

SparkIt uses submitted information to respond to inquiries, follow up on service requests, provide project support, prepare proposals, deliver creative services, improve communication, and understand what services may be helpful for your business.

If you submit information through a lead form or advertising campaign, we use that information for the purpose connected to the call-to-action you responded to, such as following up about video, social media, content, website, or marketing support.

Meta, Facebook, Instagram, and advertising leads

SparkIt may use Meta platforms, including Facebook and Instagram, to run advertising campaigns, lead forms, and promotional offers. When you submit a form through these platforms, you choose what information to send to SparkIt.

SparkIt does not knowingly target lead generation forms to minors and does not intentionally request sensitive or prohibited information through advertising forms. Please do not submit sensitive personal, medical, financial, government identification, or other highly confidential information through our website forms, Meta forms, or social media messages.

Any offer promoted through a Meta, Facebook, Instagram, or SparkIt lead form may be subject to stated offer terms, including pricing, eligibility, availability, expiration dates, service limitations, or other conditions shown at the point of collection.

Meta Pixel and website tracking

SparkIt may use Meta Pixel, Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager, and similar tools to understand website visits, page views, form activity, ad performance, and general marketing effectiveness.

These tools may collect or process information such as pages visited, device type, browser type, referral source, approximate location, interactions with ads or forms, and other usage data. This helps SparkIt improve its website, measure campaigns, and understand what content or services visitors may be interested in.

How we share information

SparkIt does not sell personal information or lead information.

SparkIt may share information only when needed to provide services, respond to your request, operate our website, process forms, manage advertising, use analytics tools, comply with legal requirements, protect the business, or work with trusted service providers who help us deliver services.

If SparkIt receives lead information on behalf of a client or advertiser, SparkIt will use that information only for the purpose connected to that client or advertiser’s campaign and will not sell, misuse, or combine that lead information with unrelated advertiser data.

Service providers

SparkIt may use third-party tools or service providers for website hosting, form handling, email, analytics, advertising, customer communication, creative production, and project management. These providers may process information only as needed to support SparkIt’s services and operations.

Data security

SparkIt takes reasonable steps to protect submitted information from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure. However, no website, form, email system, or online platform can guarantee complete security.

Your choices

You may choose not to submit a form or may contact SparkIt directly by email instead. You may also request more information about how SparkIt uses your submitted information.

You can manage certain advertising and tracking preferences through your browser settings, device settings, Google settings, Meta settings, Facebook settings, or Instagram settings.

Requesting updates or deletion

You may contact SparkIt to request access to, correction of, or deletion of information you previously submitted. SparkIt will review and respond to reasonable privacy requests as required by applicable law.

Children’s privacy

SparkIt services are intended for businesses and adults. SparkIt does not knowingly collect personal information from children or target lead generation forms to minors.

Changes to this policy

SparkIt may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised last updated date.

Contact

For privacy questions, data requests, or questions about how SparkIt uses lead information, email sparkitga@gmail.com.

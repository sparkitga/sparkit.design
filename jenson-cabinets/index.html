Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about our process and offer.

Is the design consultation really free?

Yes. We offer a free in-home or showroom consultation. We’ll measure, discuss your goals, and show options. There’s no obligation to purchase.

How long does a typical kitchen project take?

From signed agreement to completed install, most full kitchen cabinet projects run 4–8 weeks depending on scope and material lead times. We’ll give you a timeline at quote.

Do you do refacing or only new cabinets?

We do both. New custom cabinets for full remodels and new construction, and refacing/refinishing when your boxes are in good shape and you want a fresh look.

What’s included in the 10% offer?

Book a free consultation this month and, when you commit to a full kitchen or bath cabinet project, you receive 10% off the cabinet order. Details shared at your consultation.

Do you service my area?

We serve the local area from our showroom. Contact us with your city or ZIP and we’ll confirm whether we can provide in-home measure and install for you.

Can I see cabinet samples before committing?

Absolutely. During your free consultation we show wood species, door styles, and finish samples. You can also visit our showroom to see full displays.

Pricing Questions

Clear answers before you commit.

Annual credits provide flexibility across busy and slower listing seasons.

Are videos included in the subscription?

Yes. Each plan includes the annual number of videos displayed in the pricing table.

Why choose an annual plan?

Annual plans give you video credits that can be used throughout the year instead of requiring the same volume every month.

Can I purchase one video without subscribing?

Yes. One-time realtor reels are available. Email your listing photos and project details for current one-time pricing.

What happens to unused credits?

Paid-plan credits remain available during the active 12-month plan period.

What is included in the free offer?

You receive two starter listing videos so you can test the process and video style before selecting a paid plan.

Can the videos match my brand?

Paid plans can include your logo, brand colors, name, brokerage, contact information, and preferred call to action.

What types of videos can SparkIt create?

Listing launches, open houses, price updates, under-contract posts, sold posts, neighborhood highlights, and agent-brand videos.

How do I get started?

Email 3–5 listing photos, the property address, your name, brokerage information, and the action you want viewers to take.

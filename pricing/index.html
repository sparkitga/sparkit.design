Pricing Questions

Clear answers before you choose a plan.

Annual plans give your business a predictable supply of video throughout the year.

Are the videos included in the plan?

Yes. Each paid plan includes the annual number of videos displayed in the pricing table.

Why choose an annual plan?

An annual plan gives you predictable pricing and lets you use the included videos across busier and slower periods throughout the year.

Do I have to order the same number each month?

No. You can use more videos during busy months and fewer during slower months, as long as you remain within the total included in your annual plan.

What happens to videos I do not use?

Included videos are available during the active 12-month plan. Any unused portion expires when the plan term ends.

What is included in the free offer?

You receive two starter videos so you can test the SparkIt process and style before choosing a paid plan.

Can the videos match my brand?

Paid plans can include your logo, colors, name, brokerage or business information, contact details, and preferred call to action.

What types of videos can SparkIt create?

SparkIt can create listing launches, open-house videos, price updates, sold posts, service explainers, FAQs, project highlights, announcements, and brand videos.

Can I purchase one video without a plan?

One-time projects may be available depending on production needs and scheduling. Contact SparkIt for current project pricing.
